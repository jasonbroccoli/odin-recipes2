Recipe

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups cornmeal
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup of milk

Steps

  1. Crack eggs and put into bowl
  2. Add cornmeal and mix until smooth
  3. Add butter, baking powder and milk
  4. Blend with blender until smooth
  5. Pour into pan
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes
